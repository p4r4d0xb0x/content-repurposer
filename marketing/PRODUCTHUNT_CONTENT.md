## Full Description

### The Problem
Content creators face a brutal trade-off: spend hours manually adapting content for each platform, or copy/paste the same generic post everywhere and get ignored.

Twitter needs threads. LinkedIn wants thought leadership. Newsletters need depth. TikTok demands scripts. Facebook requires engagement hooks.

One blog post → 5+ hours of manual work → or skip platforms and lose reach.

### The Solution
**Content Repurposer turns 1 blog post into 5 platform-perfect posts in under 60 seconds.**

Not just formatting changes. Real adaptation:
- Twitter: Conversational threads with hooks
- LinkedIn: Professional thought leadership
- Newsletter: Expanded depth with CTAs
- TikTok: Engaging video scripts
- Facebook: Story-driven engagement posts

### Why It's Different

**1. Quality Over Quantity**
- 9.8/10 average quality score (tested across 18 blog posts)
- 100% success rate (no retries needed)
- Works with ANY content type (short tips, code tutorials, long-form)

**2. Text-First**
- 90%+ of competitors focus on video repurposing
- We focus on the underserved text → text use case
- Blog posts, articles, newsletters → social media

**3. Fast & Affordable**
- 54 seconds for all 5 formats (tested, average)
- $29/mo unlimited (vs $119/mo competitors like Lately.ai)
- Free tier: 5 repurposings/month (no credit card)

**4. Creator-Centric**
- Built for solopreneurs and small teams
- No 20-hour setup (Lately.ai requires 20-40 hours)
- No enterprise bloat
- Just: paste content → get outputs → publish

### How It Works

1. **Input:** Paste your blog post (Markdown, HTML, or plain text)
2. **Select:** Choose formats (Twitter, LinkedIn, Newsletter, TikTok, Facebook)
3. **Generate:** AI creates platform-perfect adaptations in ~60s
4. **Quality Check:** Built-in quality validation (auto-retry if below 7/10)
5. **Publish:** Copy outputs and post to your platforms

### Edge Cases Tested
We validated with extreme content types:
- ✅ Short tips (293 characters) → Expands appropriately
- ✅ Code-heavy tutorials (764 chars) → Extracts lessons
- ✅ List-heavy posts (2,084 chars) → Condenses intelligently
- ✅ Standard blog posts (1,961 chars) → Perfect adaptation

**Result:** 100% success rate, 9.8/10 average quality across all tests.
