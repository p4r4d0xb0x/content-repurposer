# Community Targets — Content Repurposer Launch

**Purpose:** Quick list of communities + angles to warm up before launch.
**Use:** Pick 2–3, post warm-up (D-3), then launch day posts.

---

## Reddit (Warm‑up → Launch)

### Primary (high intent)
- **r/SideProject** — build-in-public update, share real outputs
- **r/Entrepreneur** — “time-saver for solo founders” angle
- **r/indiehackers** — metrics + launch countdown

### Secondary (content-focused)
- **r/content_marketing** — quality over quantity framing
- **r/socialmedia** — platform-specific output angle
- **r/marketing** — productivity + consistency benefit

**Notes:**
- Warm-up: no links, ask for feedback
- Launch day: include PH link in comments, not main post
