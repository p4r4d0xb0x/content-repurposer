## 🎯 Market Gaps & Opportunities

### Gap #1: Text-First Repurposing
**Problem:** Most tools focus on video → video/clips (Opus, Pictory, Descript)  
**Opportunity:** Text → multiple text formats (blog → Twitter, LinkedIn, newsletter)  
**Why It Matters:** Faster, cheaper, larger TAM (every writer, not just video creators)

### Gap #2: Quality Over Quantity
**Problem:** Tools generate 100+ outputs (generic, low quality)  
**Opportunity:** 5 GREAT outputs (context-aware, platform-optimized)  
**Why It Matters:** Creators want quality, not spam

### Gap #3: Affordable Premium Quality
**Problem:** $10-19/mo tools = mediocre AI, $100+/mo tools = overkill for solopreneurs  
**Opportunity:** $29/mo = premium AI (Gemini/Claude) at creator-friendly price  
**Why It Matters:** Sweet spot between budget and enterprise

### Gap #4: Fast Setup
**Problem:** Lately.ai = 20-40 hours setup, steep learning curve  
**Opportunity:** 15 minutes to first output (CLI or web)  
**Why It Matters:** Creators want results NOW, not next week

### Gap #5: Creator-Focused UX
**Problem:** Enterprise tools (Lately, Repurpose.io) = complex, overwhelming  
**Opportunity:** Simple interface (CLI or web form), no bloat  
**Why It Matters:** Solopreneurs need simplicity, not 50-page dashboards
